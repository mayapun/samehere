### SameHere

SameHere is a lightweight emotional support web app that helps people feel less alone by showing them similar lived experiences and one gentle next step.

Users describe what they are going through in their own words. SameHere finds a few emotionally similar story examples from a small curated dataset, reflects back the shared feelings in a supportive tone, and offers one small action they can take right now.

It is not therapy, not diagnosis, and not crisis care. It is an early-support tool designed to reduce isolation, normalize hard feelings, and help someone take a first small step.
